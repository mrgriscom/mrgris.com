An interactive quiz/training tool for Android. An instructor uses Quizzi to pose questions to a class; Quizzi receives their answers over SMS and tabulates them in real-time. Think "clickers" but without the infrastructure and cost. The only infrastructure requirements are: students' personal cell phones, the instructor's Android phone running Quizzi, reliable cell service, and credit to cover SMS usage. A Dimagi project.
