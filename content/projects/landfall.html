If you've ever stood on a beach staring out at the ocean, wondering what's across the other side, this project is for you. Computes the first landfall in all directions from a given point. Results are presented in a panorama-esque 'first person' view, as well as more traditional sightlines viewable in Google Earth. Due to the curvature of the Earth, the results may surprise you! Powered by data from OpenStreetMap.
